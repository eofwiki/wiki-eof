# Hideout

Your [hideout](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/hideout) is your personal base and the center of your progression potential. Its [modules](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/hideout) are critical to your survival and prosperity.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/1.12 Image no 3 - Hideout.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

From an in-game economy perspective, three [modules](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/hideout) are paramount:

[**Generator:**](../game/hideout/generator.md)

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (1951).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Generator</p></figcaption></figure></div>

\
This module powers your entire [hideout](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/hideout), including the [Engine](https://wiki.eof.gg/utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores). It requires [Globs of Fuel](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/materials) to operate, creating a constant resource demand you must manage. The [generator](https://wiki.eof.gg/game/hideout/generator) itself is free, but upgrading it with in-game resources is essential for supporting efficient crafting as well as powering the Engine to keep opening Faction Lockboxes.

[**Biolab Module:**](../game/hideout/biolab.md)

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (6) (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Biolab module</p></figcaption></figure></div>

\
This module allows you to combine five [shards](../utility-nfts/faction-shards-and-lockboxes.md) of the same faction to craft a [Faction Lockbox NFT](../utility-nfts/faction-shards-and-lockboxes.md). &#x20;

[**Engine:**](../utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores.md)

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/the engine.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Engine </p></figcaption></figure></div>

\
This is the module that allows opening [Faction Lockboxes](../utility-nfts/faction-shards-and-lockboxes.md) and looting the largest amounts of in-game asset [ALLOY](https://wiki.eof.gg/economy/dual-currencies/gear). It requires an activated [Core](https://wiki.eof.gg/utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores) NFT to work.
