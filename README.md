
# Welcome to Engines of Fury!

Engines of Fury is a top-down extraction shooter that pits you against mutated horrors and, more importantly, other players. Your goal is simple: get in, grab as much as you can, and get out alive. Every raid you risk your gear for a shot at valuable in-game resources.
