# Monthly Economic Cycle

At the end of each month, the economic cycle undergoes a full reset, most importantly, the dynamic conversion rate, which returns to its most favorable starting point at 1:1. This reset is crucial for maintaining a level playing field, ensuring that every month offers a fresh and equitable opportunity for all participantsâ€”veterans and newcomers alike.

A full wipe resets all players in-game progression. The following changes will occur:

* All in-game progress will be wiped.

* FURY-to-ALLOY exchange rate will be reset to 1:1.
* All players will start from zero.
