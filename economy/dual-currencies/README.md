# ALLOY & FURY

The Engines of Fury is built on a two-tier in-game asset system designed for stability of the overall in-game economy and rewarding dedicated & skilled players. Understanding the distinction between our two key in-game assets, [ALLOY](https://wiki.eof.gg/economy/dual-currencies/gear) and [FURY](https://wiki.eof.gg/economy/dual-currencies/fury), is the first step toward mastering the game.
