# Faction Lockbox Loot & Summary

Once a Lockbox is placed in an activated [Engines Core](engine-and-its-cores.md), it will begin an 20-hour unlocking process. When complete, the lockbox yields a random but significant amount of [ALLOY](../economy/dual-currencies/alloy.md) - between 100 to 10,000. This makes mastering the [Faction Lockbox](faction-shards-and-lockboxes.md) lifecycle the primary strategy for acquiring large quantities of [ALLOY](../economy/dual-currencies/alloy.md) and a cornerstone of efficient skill of resource gathering in-game.
