# Single Use Engine Core

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**What is the Single-use Engine Core?**

Single-use Engine Core (non-NFT, expires immediately after use) is a consumable item that can be used to open one [Faction Lockbox ](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/utility-nfts/faction-shards-and-lockboxes)at the [Engine](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores) module. It can only be used once and expires immediately after use. There are no tier levels or variations of the Single-use Engine Core.\
\
**Obtaining Single-use Engine Core**

Single-use Engine Core can be purchased from the Hideout [Trading Station]([../../key-economic-hubs/trading-station.md](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/key-economic-hubs/trading-station)) for 400 [FURY](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/economy/dual-currencies/fury).(Pricing may be adjusted in future updates)\
\
**Power Requirements:**
\
Be aware that the Engine consumes a significant amount of power. Keeping your hideout's Generator upgraded and carefully planning your Glob of Fuel usage (or buying it for FURY in Trading Station is essential skill of strategy and planning for continuous operation the Engine module.&#x20;
