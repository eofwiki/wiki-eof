# Single Use Engine Core

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**What is the Single-use Engine Core?**

Single-use Engine Core (non-NFT, expires immediately after use) is a consumable item that can be used to open one [Faction Lockbox ](../../utility-nfts/faction-shards-and-lockboxes.md)at the [Engine](../../utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores.md) module. It can only be used once and expires immediately after use. There are no tier levels or variations of the Single-use Engine Core.\
\
**Obtaining Single-use Engine Core**

Single-use Engine Core can be purchased from the Hideout [Trading Station](../../key-economic-hubs/trading-station.md) for 400 [FURY](../../economy/dual-currencies/fury.md).(Pricing may be adjusted in future updates)\
\
**Power Requirements:**\
\
Be aware that [the Engine](../../utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores.md) consumes a significant amount of power. Keeping your hideout's Generator upgraded and carefully planning your Glob of Fuel usage (or buying it for[ FURY](../../economy/dual-currencies/fury.md) in [Trading Station](../../key-economic-hubs/trading-station.md)) is essential skill of strategy and planning for continuous operation the [Engine](../../utility-nfts/engine-and-its-cores.md) module.&#x20;
