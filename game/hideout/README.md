# Hideout

Hideout is the hub, where the player character can interact with different hideout modules to craft and upgrade their [equipment](../equipment.md) and other items, recycle looted [materials](../materials.md) and go to raids.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1.12 Image no 3 - Hideout.png" alt=""><figcaption><p><em>Hideout</em></p></figcaption></figure>

**Hideout Modules and their purpose:**

* Comms Station - used to interact with quests, and go to raids.
* [Recycler ](recycler.md)- used to recycle looted [junk items](../materials.md) and [equipment](../equipment.md) into raw [materials ](../materials.md)for further crafting.
* Stash - used by the player to safely store their items, to not lose them during a raid.
* [Precision Assembler](precision-assembler.md) - used to craft [equipment](../equipment.md) & [weapons](../weapons.md).
* [Mini Factory](mini-factory.md) - used to craft [equipment](../equipment.md) & [weapons](../weapons.md).
* [Biolab](biolab.md) - used to craft [equipment](../equipment.md) and [consumables](../healing-items.md).
* [Trading Station](trading-station.md) - used to exchange raw[ materials](../materials.md) for currency.
* [Mod Bench](mod-bench.md) - used to upgrade owned [equipment](../equipment.md).
* [Engine](engine.md)
* [Generator](generator.md) - The central hub that powers all other [modules](./) in the player's [hideout](./).&#x20;
