# Hideout

Hideout is the hub, where the player character can interact with different hideout modules to craft and upgrade their equipment and other items, recycle looted materials and go to raids.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/1.12 Image no 3 - Hideout.png>)

**Hideout Modules and their purpose:**

* Comms Station - used to interact with quests, and go to raids.
* Recycler - used to recycle looted juck items and equipment into raw materials for further crafting.
* Stash - used by the player to safely store their items, to not lose them during a raid.
* Precision Assembler - used to craft equipment & weapons.
* Mini Factory - used to craft equipment & weapons.
* Biolab - used to craft equipment and consumables.
* Trading Station - used to exchange raw materials for currency.
* Mod Bench - used to upgrade owned equipment.
* Engine
* Generator - The central hub that powers all other modules in the player's hideout.&#x20;
