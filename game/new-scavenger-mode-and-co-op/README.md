# New! Scavenger mode & Co-op

Two new & thrilling game features that will redefine how players explore, battle, and survive in the wasteland. [Scavenger Mode]([scavenger-mode.md](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/game/new-scavenger-mode-and-co-op/scavenger-mode)) and [Co-op Mode]([co-op.md](https://eofwiki.github.io/wiki-eof/#/game/new-scavenger-mode-and-co-op/co-op)) deliver fresh ways to experience gameplay - whether you prefer venturing out solo in raids or teaming up with allies to take on tougher challenges together.\
